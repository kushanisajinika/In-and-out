IN & OUT

Some Bay Area diners may recognize the duo behind this new venture. Chef / owners Sergio Emilio Monleón and Emily Sarlatte have gained quite a following for their incredible paellas, cooked at many high-end weddings and local food festivals with their catering company Ñora Cocina Española. In and out, the pair will continue to share their innovative take on Spanish cuisine in a space that offers a warm, lively local neighborhood vibe.
